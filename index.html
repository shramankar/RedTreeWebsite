How Credits Work

1 credit = 1 minute of video. Example: A 4-minute video = 4 credits. If you choose to edit that video, editing uses the same number of credits → another 4 credits (total 8 credits).

Unused credits carry over as long as your subscription stays active. If you upgrade, your unused credits carry over to the new plan. Your old plan ends immediately and is not refunded because credits roll over.
