About Golpo


Golpo is a revolutionary AI video creation platform developed by Red Tree Inc. Our advanced AI technology transforms your content into professional-quality videos in minutes, not hours. Founded by leaders in AI innovation, we're committed to making video creation accessible to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of quality and security.
Golpo leverages cutting-edge AI to create stunning videos from your text, images, and content. No technical skills required - simply input your content and let our AI handle the rest, all while keeping your data secure and private.
