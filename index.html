About Us


RedTree, founded by leaders in AI innovation, uses advanced language models to provide unparalleled data analysis while prioritizing security and privacy. Our experienced team, renowned for creating Community AI, continuously adapts our solutions to meet evolving business needs, fostering efficiency, productivity, and informed decision-making.
RedTree uses the power of a large language model and is designed seamlessly to access and analyze organization's documents, databases and presentations while keeping enterprise data security, privacy in mind.

Our Mission

RedTree is committed to empowering businesses through advanced AI solutions. Our mission centers on harnessing the power of large language models to access and analyze business documents, databases, and presentations. With a firm emphasis on data security and privacy, we aim to drive productivity and informed decision-making. Our continuous innovation meets evolving business needs, supported by a culture of transparency, collaboration, and customer-centricity. At RedTree, we're transforming the way organizations interact with and understand their data, offering a future-focused approach to business intelligence.
